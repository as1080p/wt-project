Director Spotlight

Rajkumar Hirani

Master of Social Commentary

Rajkumar Hirani is an Indian film director and editor. He is widely regarded as one of the most successful and influential filmmakers in Indian cinema, known for his ability to blend humor with social messages.

Born: November 20, 1962 in Nagpur, Maharashtra, India

Notable Films:

  • Munna Bhai M.B.B.S. (2003)
  • 3 Idiots (2009)
  • PK (2014)
  • Sanju (2018)

Awards: 5 National Film Awards, 6 Filmfare Awards
