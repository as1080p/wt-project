Actor Spotlight

Shah Rukh Khan

The King of Bollywood

Shah Rukh Khan, also known as SRK, is one of the most successful and influential actors in Indian cinema. He has appeared in more than 80 Hindi films and has won numerous awards, including 14 Filmfare Awards.

Born: November 2, 1965 in New Delhi, India

Notable Films:

  • Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (1995)
  • My Name is Khan (2010)
  • Chennai Express (2013)
  • Chak De! India (2007)

Awards: 14 Filmfare Awards, Padma Shri (2005)
